IMPORTANT NOTICE:

June 13, 2022
Country Bank for Savings, Ware, Massachusetts has petitioned the Massachusetts Division of Banks for permission to close the branch office at 75 Main Street, Ware, MA 01082. Current Main Street customers will be serviced at our 155 West Street, Ware, MA 01082 location. Any objections or comments relative to this proposal must be communicated in writing to the Commissioner of Banks, Mary Gallagher, Office of the Commissioner of Banks, 1000 Washington Street, 10th Floor, Boston, MA 02118, no later than two weeks from the date of the posting of this notice. The branch is proposed to close on November 30, 2022, and customers may obtain services at 155 West Street, Ware, MA 01082.

July 27, 2022
COUNTRY BANK FOR SAVINGS MAIN OFFICE RELOCATION: 155 WEST ST., WARE, MA 01082
Country Bank for Savings, a Massachusetts chartered mutual savings bank with its Main Office currently located at 75 Main Street, Ware, Massachusetts 01082, is applying with the Federal Deposit Insurance Corporation for permission to relocate the Main Office to 155 West St., Ware, MA 01082. Any person wishing to comment on this application may file his or her comments in writing with the regional director of the Federal Deposit Insurance Corporation at the appropriate FDIC office located 15 Braintree Hill Park, Suite 200, Braintree, MA 02184-8701 not later than 15 days from the date of this publication. The non-confidential portions of the application are on file at the appropriate FDIC office and are available for public inspection during regular business hours. Photocopies of the non-confidential portion of the application file will be made available upon request
