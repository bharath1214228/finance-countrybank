We believe in making life easier

Wherever you go, and whenever you need us, these tools ensure we’re with you.

Imagine having us in your pocket, available 24 hours a day to serve you. That’s why we offer mobile and online banking so you can bank when and where you need to. From your smartphone, or tablet, you can pay bills, turn your debit card on and off, deposit checks, view your accounts, transfer money between accounts and pay people, open an account and more!

    Mobile Wallet

    Country Bank supports mobile wallet payments with Apple Pay, Samsung Pay and Google Pay. You can simply scan your phone to make payments anywhere mobile purchasing services are accepted. Here’s how mobile purchasing works:

    1. Open your mobile wallet app on your iPhone (for Apple Pay) or download the Samsung Pay app or Google Pay app on your phone, and then add your Country Bank Mastercard® debit card information.
    2. At checkout, hold up your device to the scanner to make a purchase. Since your card is digitally encrypted, there’s an extra layer of security every time you choose to pay with your phone.
    3. That’s it! Go on your merry way, without even reaching for your wallet.

    Chip Cards (AKA EMV Cards)

    A chip (or EMV) card has an embedded microchip that provides enhanced security and protection against fraud.  Card information on the chip is encrypted and creates a single-use code which makes it extremely difficult to counterfeit.  The chip is on the front of the card while the magnetic strip is still on the back of the card so you are able to use your card at chip-enabled and magnetic strip terminals.

    Europay, Mastercard® and Visa® (EMV) established chip card standards so they are commonly used around the world making them easier to use outside of the U.S. as well as, in the U.S. as more merchants move to these standards.

    How to use your chip card at chip-enabled merchants or ATMs

    Each ATM or merchant terminal may be different so the rule of thumb is to “follow the prompts.” You will be prompted to re-insert your chip card after swiping your magnetic strip and most terminals and ATMS require you to leave your card in the terminal until the transaction is complete.

  • Open an Account Online A2A and P2P Transfers

    Open an Account Online

    Opening an account has never been so easy! Open an account online today!

    • Available to individuals in MA, NH, ME, RI, CT, VT and all existing customers
    • Must be at least 18 years old to open an account online
    • Several options to fund your new account
    • Use our uSwitch kit to easily transfer direct deposits, automatic payments and accounts from other institutions
    • Open an account through our website, or online banking
    • Order a Mastercard® debit or ATM card
    • Order checks

    A2A and P2P Transfers for consumers…Send and Receive money from people and external accounts!

    Available through online banking or the mobile app.

    P2P – Person to Person Transfer: Send a payment to or request money from another person. All you need is their email address or mobile phone number.

    • Email address – recipient will receive an email requesting permission to receive or send funds. Recipient will follow a hyperlink where they will enter their account information.
    • Mobile phone number – recipient will receive a text message requesting permission to receive or send funds. Recipient will follow a hyperlink where they will enter their account information.

    A2A – Account to Account Transfer: Move money between your Country Bank accounts and your accounts at other financial institutions. You must be an owner on both accounts.

    • Enter the routing and account numbers of your external account. The name on the account must be the primary account owner.
    • Go back to your “transfer” tab and then “send and receive” and enter the trial transaction amounts to get started.

    Eligible accounts include consumer checking, savings and money market deposit accounts.

    To confirm your account, two trial transactions less than $1 will credit and debit your account at the other institution within two business days.
